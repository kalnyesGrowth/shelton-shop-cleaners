Our Story

Family Owned. Community Trusted.

David Park and his son opened Shelton Shop Cleaners in 1997 with a simple promise: treat every garment like it was their own. Nearly three decades later, that commitment has not changed.

As a family-owned and operated business in the heart of Stafford, we have built lasting relationships with our neighbors, military families, and professionals throughout the community. Many of our customers have been with us for 10, 15, even 20+ years.

We are proud to be a multiple-year recipient of the "Stafford's Best" award for the best dry cleaners in Stafford County. That recognition comes from our customers, and it means everything to us.

How It Works

Simple, Reliable Process

Getting your garments professionally cleaned has never been easier.

Step 01

Drop Off

Bring your garments to our convenient Garrisonville Road location. We are open Monday through Saturday with extended weekday hours.

Step 02

Inspect & Tag

We carefully inspect each item, noting any stains, damage, or special instructions. Every garment is tagged and tracked.

Step 03

Clean & Press

Professional cleaning using the right method for each fabric. Your clothes are pressed to perfection and packaged with care.

Step 04

Pick Up

Your garments are ready on time, every time. We will have everything clean, pressed, and waiting for you.
